Who are we?

about us


Home2Host is a company established with the purpose of providing professional property management for short-term rentals. Renting out properties through platforms such as Airbnb, Booking, and others is becoming increasingly popular and preferred. This allows property owners not only to earn additional income from their property but also to use it whenever needed.
…

However, this activity requires time and effort – cleaning, communication with guests, welcoming, check-in, and price adjustment. Home2Host takes care of all these responsibilities and manages your property comprehensively, focusing on optimization and maximizing your income.

We handle the guests, you enjoy the rest!
